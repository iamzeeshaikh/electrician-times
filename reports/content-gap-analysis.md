## Biggest gaps (chosen for the initial batch of 10)

1. **Protective-device comparisons** — readers land on RCD/breaker posts but the site never explains
   RCD vs RCBO vs GFCI terminology across regions. High search intent, strong internal-link hub.
2. **Troubleshooting intent** — "why does my breaker keep tripping", "why do my LEDs flicker":
   classic informational queries the site can rank for with its existing topical authority.
3. **Safety fundamentals** — extension-cord safety, a homeowner safety checklist. Aligns with the
   site's safety category and lets us cite authoritative bodies (HSE, ESFI, Electrical Safety First).
4. **International standards framing** — existing grounding/color posts assume post-Soviet PUE
   standards. Comparison articles (wire colors by region, earthing system types TN/TT/IT,
   aluminum vs copper) give the content international relevance without touching the old URLs.
5. **Beginner/apprentice angle** — tools for apprentice electricians complements existing tool
   reviews and the "Write for Us" audience.

## Deliberately NOT created
- Location/service pages (mass thin pages — prohibited).
- Anything requiring current code citations (NEC/BS 7671 edition specifics) that cannot be
  verified — articles reference standards bodies generically and tell readers to verify locally.
- News/time-sensitive content that would age immediately.
