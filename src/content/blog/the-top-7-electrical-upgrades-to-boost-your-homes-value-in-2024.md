<h2 dir="ltr">Upgrade your fuse box or consumer unit</h2>
<p dir="ltr">If your home has an outdated fuse box, upgrading to a modern consumer unit is essential for safety and compliance with current regulations. Modern units include Residual Current Devices (RCDs) that protect against electric shocks and reduce the risk of electrical fires.</p>
<p dir="ltr">H3: Signs You Need an Upgrade:</p>
<ul>
<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">Frequent circuit breaker trips.</p>
</li>
<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">A fuse box with outdated fuses instead of switches.</p>
</li>
<li dir="ltr">
<p dir="ltr" role="presentation">Adding new appliances or systems to your home.</p>
</li>
</ul>
