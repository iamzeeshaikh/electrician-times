<p>In the process of performing electrical wiring, every master is necessarily faced with such an activity as connecting sections with a wire to each other.&nbsp;The most common electrical wiring fault is a short circuit.&nbsp;Also, a very common unpleasant occurrence is a circuit break, most often it happens at the junction of the wires.&nbsp;These connections are made in and mounted in the plane of the wall or on its surface.&nbsp;Often inside these boxes, there are wire connections that lead to the automatic distribution panel, as well as wires that go to outlets and lights.&nbsp;Another wire can be laid parallel to them, which connects the boxes.</p>

<h2>Terminal Blocks</h2>

<p>The use of this switching option is the most popular due to the minimal labor costs for installation and compliance with all standards.&nbsp;A separate advantage is the ability to connect wires of different sections and materials.&nbsp;These days, the market for electrical materials is crowded with various products, but these terminal blocks are undoubtedly the best sellers.</p>

<h2>Types of wire terminal connections</h2>

<p>Terminal clamps allow you to eliminate contact between wires made of different materials in the connection of wires.&nbsp;Today, the leaders in the field of electrical connections are 3 types of terminal blocks:</p>

<ol><li>Spring type.</li>

<li>Screw type.</li>

<li>Knife type.</li>
</ol>

<p>The pads are mainly made of brass.&nbsp;They consist of assemblies of 2 or more terminals.&nbsp;Some varieties are filled with a specialized gel to protect against external corrosive influences.&nbsp;They differ from each other in: the diameter of the connecting cores, voltage and current.</p>

<p>Currently, this type of wire connection, such as ordinary twisting, has begun to fade into the background.&nbsp;To ensure reliable contact at the junctions of electrical wiring, specialized connecting terminals are used, which are called terminal blocks.&nbsp;There is a huge variety of such pads on the modern market in our country.&nbsp;They differ from each other: in price tag, design and some performance features.<br>The design of the pads is identical.&nbsp;It consists of the body itself, inside of which there are special cells, and inside these cells there are already brass tubes with several threaded holes.</p>

<p>The tubes themselves come in different sections; they are selected according to their diameter, corresponding to the cross-sectional diameter of the wires themselves.<br>Terminal blocks are used in a wide variety of places.&nbsp;An example would be the way to connect an ordinary chandelier in an apartment; it is connected to short conductors sticking out of a hole in the ceiling.</p>

<h2>Connecting wires using terminal blocks</h2>

<p>Choosing a block for connecting wires is a responsible action.&nbsp;It is necessary to pay attention to the strength of the current that will subsequently flow through this product.&nbsp;It is also necessary to purchase the required number of mounting terminals.<br>The process itself is not a complicated procedure; even amateurs can carry it out if they have the initial necessary knowledge and skills.<br>The block is selected according to the size of the cell, all excess sections are cut off.&nbsp;Most often they are made with terminal blocks.<br>Having previously removed the conductors from the connecting ends, the wire cores are inserted into the terminal cells and all conductors are clamped using screws.&nbsp;The surface of the core must be cleaned.&nbsp;A slight amount of force is allowed when tightening the screws.<br>In the case of connecting aluminium conductors, the screws are tightened very carefully, without much pressure.&nbsp;This is done to avoid breaking the wire and deteriorating the contact in the future since this metal has high fluidity when heated.</p>
