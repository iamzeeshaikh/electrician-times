<h2>Solar Panel Degradation Rate</h2>
<p>Solar panel degradation rate refers to the gradual decrease in efficiency and power output over time, typically around 0.5% per year. Factors like sunlight, temperature fluctuations, humidity, and material quality contribute to this decline. After 25 years, panels usually retain about 87.5% of their original capacity. However, improvements in technology have reduced degradation rates, ensuring better long-term performance.</p>
