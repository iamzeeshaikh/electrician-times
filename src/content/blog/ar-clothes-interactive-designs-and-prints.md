<h2>How AR Clothes Work</h2>
<p>AR clothes utilize augmented reality technology to create interactive experiences. These garments are embedded with patterns, codes, or markers that can be scanned using a smartphone or AR device. Once scanned, these elements activate animations, visual effects, or even digital overlays, transforming an ordinary outfit into an extraordinary experience.</p>
<h3>Technologies Behind AR Clothes</h3>
<ul data-spread="false">
<li><strong>Augmented Reality Apps</strong>: Custom AR applications act as the bridge between the physical garment and the digital experience.</li>
<li><strong>Wearable Markers</strong>: Unique patterns or QR codes on the clothes trigger AR effects.</li>
<li><strong>3D Modeling Software</strong>: Creates dynamic designs and animations visible through AR devices.</li>
</ul>
