<h3>Identifying Genuine Cables</h3>
<p>To identify genuine cables, look for certifications and compliance markings. Reputable manufacturers provide detailed specifications and test reports. Ensuring that cables are sourced from authorized distributors further guarantees their authenticity.</p>
<h3>Risks of Counterfeit Cables</h3>
<p>Counterfeit cables often lack the necessary quality and safety standards. They may have substandard insulation, inadequate conductor materials, and poor construction. These deficiencies can result in overheating, electrical fires, and system failures.</p>
