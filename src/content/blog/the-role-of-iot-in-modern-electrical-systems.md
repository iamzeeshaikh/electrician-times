<h2><strong><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;">How IoT Is Revolutionizing Electrical Systems</span></strong></h2>
<p><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;">Electrical systems have traditionally relied on reactive maintenance and manual inspections. This approach often leads to costly downtime, safety hazards, and inefficiencies. IoT introduces a paradigm shift, enabling real-time monitoring, data-driven decisions, and proactive maintenance.</span></p>
<p><span lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12.0pt;">IoT sensors embedded in electrical systems can monitor parameters such as voltage, current, temperature, and humidity. These sensors continuously transmit data to a centralized platform, where advanced analytics transform raw data into actionable insights. This process allows technicians to identify potential issues before they escalate, ensuring system reliability and reducing downtime.</span></p>
